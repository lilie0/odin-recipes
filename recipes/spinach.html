spinach salad recipe

spinach salad

sigeumchi-namul is an easy and healthy dish that can be made within a few minutes. feel free to adjust the recipe to your liking.

ingredients

  • spinach (use a lot, it shrinks when blanched)
  • dash of soy sauce
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • drizzle sesame oil
  • drizzle of chilli oil
  • sesame seeds and scallions (optional toppings)

steps

  1. prepare ice bath for spinach.
  2. bring a pot of lightly salted water to boil. cook spinach until slightly wilted (around 30 seconds to a minute)
  3. transfer spinach to ice bath. drain once chilled and lightly wring out water from the spinach with your hands.
  4. using a cutting board, cut spinach into bite sized pieces.
  5. in a bowl, combine spinach with the soy sauce, garlic and oils.
  6. when serving, sprinkle scallions and sesame seeds on top. enjoy!
